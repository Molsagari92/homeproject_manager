Welcome to the MANAGER game!

Your goal is to double the assets of your company which is a factory what produces jam.

Here are the rules:

  • Your starter amount of is: 1 000 000 HUF.
  • The salary of a worker for a month: 50 000 HUF.
  • A worker can produce 30 units of product in a month.
  • One round represents a month.
  • The goal is to achieve 2 000 000 HUF during the game.

And here are the actions:

  • You can buy materials: in this case your materials are fruits and sugar.
  • You can hire or fire workers.
  • You can sell the products. Attention: the maximal price is 250% of the production cost.
  • You can advertise your factory's products, it improves the sales but it costs money.
  • You can repeat the steps above until your resources are enough.
  • After that you have to close the month and everything starts again.
